Program Description

Rosemount Thunder is one of the premier youth softball programs in the Twin Cities metro area. We offer competitive travel ball for ages 10U through 16U with a focus on player development, teamwork, and academic excellence. Our staff includes former college players and certified coaches dedicated to helping every athlete reach their full potential.
